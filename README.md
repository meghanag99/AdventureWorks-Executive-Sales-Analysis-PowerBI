# AdventureWorks-Executive-Sales-Analysis-PowerBI
A multi-page Power BI dashboard analyzing sales performance, customer behavior, market opportunities, returns, and seasonality using the AdventureWorks dataset.

## Executive Summary

This project is a professional Power BI business intelligence dashboard built using the AdventureWorks dataset.

The dashboard provides a structured view of company performance through five analytical lenses:

- Financial performance  
- Customer behavior and retention  
- Regional growth opportunities  
- Operational efficiency and returns  
- Seasonality and revenue trends  

# Tools Used

- PowerBI (DAX, Power Query, Data Visulaization)
---

# Project Objective

Organizations generate large volumes of sales and operational data. Decision-makers need clear answers to critical business questions such as:

- Is the business growing profitably?
- Which customers are most valuable?
- Which markets should be prioritized for expansion?
- Where are returns reducing profitability?
- What seasonal trends should guide planning?

This dashboard was designed to answer those questions.

# Dashboard Structure & Key Findings

## 1. Executive Overview

High-level performance view.

- **$24.9M Total Revenue** with **$10.5M Profit** and a strong **42.0% Profit Margin**, indicating healthy business performance.
- **25K Total Orders** were processed across the reporting period.
- **166.2% YoY Growth** shows strong acceleration in later years.
- Bikes generated the highest profit contribution across product categories.
- Revenue was concentrated across a small number of major markets.

---

## 2. Customer Insights & Segmentation

Customer value, loyalty, and demographic analysis.

- **17K Active Customers** generated an average of **$1.4K revenue per customer**.
- **33.6% Repeat Customer Rate** shows moderate loyalty with room for retention improvement.
- Customers placed **1.4 average orders each**.
- Australia had the highest customer value despite a smaller customer base.
- United States had the largest customer base but lower spend per customer.
- Customers aged **50–59** were the highest-value segment.
- Medium-income customers contributed the highest overall revenue.

---

## 3. Regional Growth & Market Opportunities

Market performance and expansion analysis.

- **United States** was the top revenue market.
- **Canada** delivered the highest profit margin performance.
- **Germany** showed the strongest expansion opportunity.
- Top two markets contributed **61.6% of revenue**, highlighting concentration risk.
- Smaller markets may offer efficient future growth opportunities.

---

## 4. Operational Efficiency & Returns

Returns monitoring and profit leakage analysis.

- Overall return rate remained low at **2.2%**.
- Average monthly returns were **61 units**.
- Returns created an estimated **$318.9K profit loss**.
- Certain products drove disproportionately high return volumes.
- Some high-revenue subcategories also showed elevated return risk.
- Reducing returns in top-selling products would improve margins.

---

## 5. Seasonality & Time Intelligence

Monthly trends and demand patterns.

- **Q2 2017** was the best-performing quarter.
- **June** was the highest-performing month.
- Average monthly revenue was **$1.0M**.
- Revenue softened mid-year before recovering later.
- Monthly growth rates were volatile, indicating seasonal demand shifts.
- Profit margins remained stable despite revenue fluctuations.
